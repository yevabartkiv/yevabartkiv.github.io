Idm Configurator

Configurator is a web form used to determine the optimal configuration of IdM application and its price (Configurator is on developing phase now).

You have enterprise Tasks in the data security field. For sure all those Tasks are accomplished by different people so we provide you the way to combine all Tasks that could be done within IdM application to Roles. Your enterprise Tasks may be assigned to Roles differently so we assume one person may play in more than one our Roles. In any case Tasks remain Tasks and to have data secured we recommend to review consequentially all our Roles and their tasks.
  1. Choose the first Role to get Role specific Tasks
    1. Choose the Role specific Tasks you are going to accomplish with IdM application
  2. Choose next Role to get Role specific Tasks
    1. Choose the Role specific Tasks you are going to accomplish with IdM application
  3. Choose your next Role to get Role specific Tasks
    1. Choose the Role specific Tasks you are going to accomplish with IdM application
  4. Fill in amount of users whose access rights you are going to manage
  5. Go back to the Roles, review Tasks and add more of them if needed.
  6. Choose to what data type (database) and to what software you are going to give users access rights
  7. Click Create IdM configuration to get the application description and its total price
