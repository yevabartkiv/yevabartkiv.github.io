How to use Knowledge Book

Use this step-by-step guidance to get accurate search results or to get step-by-step guidance to your specific Task.

  1. Get the section Understanding k-Book concept learned
  2. Log in with your role permission (skip this step as far as log in function is on developing phase)
  3. Go to User Roles section and choose your Role
    Sales Person
  4. Review your Role Tasks List and choose a Task you are to accomplish
    t7001. Project Kickoff
  5. Click on Task Title Name
  6. Follow the step-by-step guidance until the Task is done
